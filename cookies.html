Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of text sent by your web browser by a website you visit. A cookie file is stored in your web browser and allows the Service or a third-party to recognize you and make your next visit easier and the Service more useful to you.

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

2. How We Use Cookies

When you use and access the Service, we may place a number of cookies files in your web browser. We use cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

To enable certain functions of the Service, to provide authentication, and to remember your preferences.

Analytics Cookies

To collect information about how you use our website, which pages you visited and which links you clicked on.

Marketing Cookies

To deliver advertising that is relevant to your interests and to measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Third-Party Cookies

To enable features from third-party services such as social media platforms and analytics providers.

4. What Are Your Choices Regarding Cookies

If you'd like to delete cookies or instruct your web browser to delete or refuse cookies, please visit the help pages of your web browser.

Please note, however, that if you delete cookies or refuse to accept them, you might not be able to use all of the features we offer, you may not be able to store your preferences, and some of our pages might not display properly.

How to manage cookies in major browsers:

  • Chrome: Chrome Cookie Settings
  • Safari: Safari Cookie Settings
  • Firefox: Firefox Cookie Settings
  • Edge: Edge Cookie Settings

5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, we will ask for your consent to use cookies. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. If you choose to decline, some features of the website may not function properly.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking on the "Cookie Settings" button at the bottom of our website.
